2. WORK HOURS AND SCHEDULE
================================================================================

Standard Work Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday

Core Hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (all employees should be available during this time)

Flexible Schedule: Employees may adjust their start and end times with manager
approval, as long as core hours are covered.

Breaks:
- Lunch: 1 hour (unpaid)
- Short breaks: Two 15-minute breaks per day (paid)

3. REMOTE WORK POLICY
================================================================================

Work Model: Hybrid

Office Days: 3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday recommended)
Remote Days: 2 days per week (Monday and Friday recommended)

Flexibility: Employees can choose which specific days to work remotely, subject
to team coordination and manager approval.

Equipment Provided:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or equivalent)
- External monitor
- Keyboard and mouse
- $500 home office stipend for desk, chair, and accessories

Requirements for Remote Work:
- Reliable high-speed internet connection
- Quiet workspace free from distractions
- Availability during core hours
- Use of company VPN for all work activities

4. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
================================================================================

Annual PTO: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)

Sick Leave: 10 days per year (separate from PTO)

Holidays: The company observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (July 4th)
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day and the day after
- Christmas Day

Carryover: Up to 5 unused PTO days can be carried over to the next calendar year.
Days beyond this limit will be forfeited.

Requesting Time Off:
1. Submit request through HR system at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approval required
3. Ensure adequate coverage for your responsibilities

6. IT SECURITY POLICY
================================================================================

Device Security:
- All company devices must have full-disk encryption enabled
- Automatic screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Keep operating system and software up to date

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Change password every 90 days
- No password reuse for last 5 passwords

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
- Required for all company systems
- Use authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
- Backup codes must be stored securely

VPN Usage:
- Required when working remotely
- Required when using public Wi-Fi
- Always-on VPN recommended

Data Protection:
- Never share credentials with anyone
- Don't store sensitive data on personal devices
- Use encrypted communication channels for sensitive information
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately

Incident Reporting:
- Report security incidents to IT immediately
- Email: security@company.com
- Phone: (555) 123-4567 (24/7 hotline)

Prohibited Activities:
- Sharing company data with unauthorized parties
- Using company resources for personal business
- Installing unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate content
